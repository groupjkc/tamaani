How much does it cost?
The Basic service is $49.95 + taxes per month. This includes a daily limit of 250 Mb at a download speed of 256 kbs and an upload speed of 96 kbs.
The Power User service is $69.95 + taxes per month. This includes a daily limit of 500 Mb at a download speed of 512 kbs and an upload speed of 128 kbs.

How do I pay for it?
You can pay by credit card, direct deposit, cooperative cheque or money order. Tamaani also offers Internet banking for customers with a Caisse Desjardins account.

Do I have to buy the modem?
No, but Tamaani does charge a security deposit of $100 when you sign up, refundable upon the return of the modem in good working condition.

How does it work?

What do I need to use it?
You will need a standard PC computer (Windows 98SE, ME, 2000, XP or a Macintosh) or laptop with an Ethernet interface. Most PCs and Macintosh have Ethernet already built in. If your PC doesn't have Ethernet, ask our local agent to install one for you.

Do I need an antenna or a dish on my house?
No. The modem doesn't need an antenna. It works a bit like a cell phone, so all you need to do is connect it to your computer's Ethernet interface. It should work in any room that has a window.

Will it be as fast as "High Speed" in the South?
No, unfortunately not. It will be about 5 to 10 times faster than a dial up connection to the South but a bit slower than Southern "High Speed". Everyone who has tried it so far has found it much better than dial-up.

Can I use the phone while I'm on the Internet?
Yes. The Internet service doesn't use the phone at all so you can talk and surf at the same time!

What is 500 Megs anyway? What does that mean?
500 Megs refers to the amount of usage of the Internet. With 500 Megs you can:
-- Send or receive about 25000 emails per month! (with no pictures or attachments)
-- Visit about 1000 web sites per month!
-- Send or receive about 5000 low quality pictures, 500 high quality pictures or 100 very high quality pictures per month.
-- Listen to 20 hours of high quality or up to 80 hours of low quality Internet Radio per month (that's about 45 minute to 3 hours depending on the quality)
-- Download music for about 6 hours per month (this is NOT recommended!)
Note: The above are approximations. The actual amount will vary from person to person.

I want more than 500 Megs!
Not available.

What happens after I use up my daily limit (250 Mb for the Basic service or 500 Mb for the Power User service)?
Tamaani has a Fair Access Policy that allows users to access 250 Mb (Basic service) or 500 Mb (Power User service) per day. This policy is in place to ensure that all Tamaani customers have access to the Internet. When you exceed this limit, Tamaani automatically slows down your Internet speed for a period of 24 hours. After this period, service returns to normal.

How can I get the most for my 500 Megs?
-- Avoid downloading music and movies
-- Use the low quality setting on your digital camera for pictures you want to email
-- Turn your computer off when you aren't using it
-- Don't leave Internet radio on when you don't need it

What can I do with this service?

Will I get an email address?
Yes. Each home will have one email address. Extra email addresses are available for $5 +tax per month. Power users will get up to 3 email addresses.

Can I shop online?
Yes! You can shop online as long as the web site where you shop delivers to Nunavik. The web site will usually tell you how much they charge for shipping.

Can I bank online?
Yes! If your bank supports online banking you can use the Internet to pay your bills, view your account balance and transfer money. Ask your bank about online banking.

Can I do video conference?
Tamaani is in the process of bringing professional-grade videoconferencing to all Nunavik communities. This service is scheduled to be available by the end of the summer of 2010.

Can I download movies and music?
Yes. But remember that you have a daily download limit (250 Mb for the Basic service and 500 Mb for the Power User service). Downloading movies will use up your daily limit very quickly. Please note that peer-to-peer (p2p) websites like LimeWire and BitTorrent are prohibited and may result in your service being suspended.

Can I play Videogames online?
You can play locally. As long as you play with someone in your community, videogames will work well. If you play with someone in another community or in the South, your game will probably not work very well.

Can I email pictures I took with my digital camera?
Yes, but remember that high quality pictures take more of your 500 Megs than low quality pictures. Use low quality pictures for email and high quality pictures for printing.

Can I listen to Internet Radio?
Yes but remember that high quality will use up your 500 Megs faster than low quality. Mono will also take less than stereo. Remember to turn it off when you aren't listening.
