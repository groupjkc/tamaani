Tamaani Internet
10 year timeline 2004-2014

2004-2014

Ten years ago, the Kativik Regional Government (KRG) launched Tamaani Internet, the first region-wide Internet service provider in Nunavik. Thanks to this effort, the Internet has become a vital communication tool in the region’s home, businesses, schools and organizations. The use of the Internet is now ubiquitous and plays an important role in many aspects of community life, such as health care, remote educational opportunities, the administration of justice, as well as the connectivity of residents and the promotion of the Inuktitut language and Inuit identity.
